# Player Guide

Welcome to the Phaser Platformer! This guide will help you get started with the game.

## Controls

- **Arrow Keys** or **WASD**: Move your character left/right and jump.
- **Space**: Jump (alternative).
- **Shift**: Run / dash.
- **E**: Interact with objects (open doors, pick up items).
- **Esc**: Pause menu.

## Gameplay Basics

### Objective
Navigate through levels, defeat enemies, collect items, and reach the goal flag.

### Health & Lives
Your character has a health bar. Taking damage reduces health; when it reaches zero, you lose a life. You start with three lives.

### Items
- **Coins**: Collect for points.
- **Power‑ups**: Grant temporary abilities (speed, invincibility, double jump).
- **Keys**: Unlock doors.

### Enemies
Avoid or defeat enemies by jumping on them or using attacks.

## Multiplayer

The game supports real‑time multiplayer via Socket.IO. You can:
- Join public rooms.
- Create private rooms with friends.
- Compete on leaderboards.

## Tips

1. Use double jumps to reach higher platforms.
2. Watch for moving platforms and traps.
3. Save your progress by reaching checkpoints.
4. Team up with other players to tackle difficult levels.

## Troubleshooting

If you experience connection issues:
- Ensure your internet is stable.
- Check that the game server is online.
- Refresh the page and try again.

For more help, contact support.
